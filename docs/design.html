Do's and Don'ts

  • Bind every size, gap, corner, and opacity to a token — no raw px/hex in widgets.
  • Keep one type family (Manrope) throughout; vary voice with size/weight, not a second font.
  • Give every card the same border + radius + padding skin; vary by content only.
  • Let spacing group content — tight within, generous between.
  • Design each mode on its own terms; verify AA contrast in both.
  • No shadows, glows, or halos — elevation is border-only.
  • No second accent, purple/blue gradient, or decorative use of semantics.
  • No off-scale radius (no 18px), uniform spacing, or cramped padding.
  • Don't introduce a second font family.
  • No emoji as icons/markers; don't hardcode the 0.35 horizon opacity.
