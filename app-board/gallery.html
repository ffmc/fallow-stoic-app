Fallow

A quiet space to find Stoic perspective on what you're going through. Browse a problem that resonates — anxiety, anger, grief, self-doubt, and more — and each of the 12 categories surfaces a reflective prompt to sit with, plus a handful of passages from Stoic writers.

The prompt and passages rotate on every visit, so returning to the same situation often surfaces something new. Bookmark any passage to save it for later — everything stays on your device, no accounts, no tracking. A look at every screen of the app.
